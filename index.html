Origin-Story

Environmental healing journey in Djanan

At the start of his work in Djanan, Madjid Abdellaziz faced a challenging environment of sand, heat, DOR, snakes, and scorpions. In order to revitalize the barren land and promote environmental healing, he began with energizing the soil using Agni Hotra, which showed promising results. By adopting an old Druid custom, Madjid also worked on harmonizing the soil by tapping and turning flat stones on the site. With time, the radioactive atmosphere weakened, and nature began to recover.

2003 marked the founding of DJANAN, an ambitious project aimed at transforming an arid and barren landscape into a thriving and sustainable oasis. The project was the brainchild of a group of environmentalists, architects, and engineers who were inspired by the idea of using technology and innovation to heal the environment.
